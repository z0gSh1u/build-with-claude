Here is a one-day meal plan for a vegetarian athlete training for a marathon with three running sessions:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (130g), Fat (85g), Carbs (420g)

**Meal Plan:**

* **Pre-Dawn Breakfast (5:30 AM - Before 10km run):** Oatmeal (70g dry weight) with banana (120g), sunflower seeds (20g), and honey (15g). Low-fat milk (240ml).
  * Protein: 12g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 65g

* **Post-Run Snack (7:30 AM - After 10km run):** Greek yogurt (200g, non-fat) with granola (40g) and berries (80g).
  * Protein: 20g, Fat: 3g, Carbs: 45g

* **Mid-Morning Meal (9:30 AM):** Whole wheat toast (2 slices, 80g) with hummus (50g), sliced tomato (80g), and cucumber (60g). Side of lentil soup (250ml).

* **Pre-Afternoon Run Snack (1:30 PM - Before 8km tempo run):** Rice cakes (30g) with peanut butter alternative (sunflower seed butter, 30g) and apple (150g).
  * Protein: 10g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 50g

* **Post-Run Recovery Meal (4:00 PM - After 8km tempo run):** Chickpea and vegetable stir-fry with tofu (150g), bell peppers (100g), broccoli (120g), and brown rice (80g cooked). Olive oil (10ml).
  * Protein: 25g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 65g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM - Before evening 5km easy run):** Baked sweet potato (200g) with black bean chili (250ml, prepared with tomatoes, beans, onion, and spices). Side salad with mixed greens (100g) and balsamic vinaigrette (15ml).

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM - After easy run):** Whole grain crackers (35g) with cottage cheese (150g, low-fat) and berries (80g).
  * Protein: 27g, Fat: 4g, Carbs: 35g
